About Me

I am a final year graduate student in the Institute of Computational and Mathematical Engineering (ICME) at Stanford University, where I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Kay Giesecke and Prof. Yinyu Ye. Before coming to Stanford, I graduated from the University of Michigan - Ann Arbor with a degree in mathematics. Starting from August 2022, I will be an Assistant Professor at the Department of Statistic and Operations Research at the University of North Carolina - Chapel Hill.


Research interests: Sequential decision making and online algorithms; Optimization and applications in operations research; Simulation and applied probability
